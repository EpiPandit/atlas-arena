# _Sigmodon alstoni_

## Common Name
Altson's cotton rat

## Host for Diseases:
Venezuelan Hemorrhagic Fever (Guanarito Virus)

## IUCN Status
Least Concern

## Population Trend
Unknown

## Reproduction
Litter size: unconfirmed reports of 3-7 pups\
Gestation: 27 days

## Range: 
Brazil, Colombia, Guyana, Suriname, Venezuela

## Habitat: 
Shrublands, grasslands, open crop fields on low elevation lands

## Epidemiology
This rodent spreads the Guanarito Virus primarily to agircultural workers, with cases peaking from November to January.

## Other Information
Most activity of the rats can be observed during daytime (diurnal).
